About

I'm an Emmy Award winning Producer and multi-award winning Writer & Director with experience in feature-length films, shorts, documentaries, music videos, commercials, and branded content.

I decided to create a personal production company because my work consistently delivers high-quality, award-winning content that reaches millions of viewers, this includes 3x International MARCOM Awards for advertising/marketing social media campaigns.

My background in sales, mechanical engineering, and assistant directing affords me a unique ability to adapt to any environment that a company requires in order to develop content that creates an impact and drives awareness. My active curiosity in understanding a client’s core principles and goals enables me to develop scripts, pitch decks, and more, to create engaging content that maximizes client ROI.

I'm always interested in learning more about different companies, so reach out, and help me share what you want to say.
